Trains are cool

Have you ever looked up above MTCC as the Green Line whooshes by? Isn't it awesome how we live in a city as great as Chicago and have easy access to cheap, reliable public transportation?

Few Americans live in walking distance of public transit, we should appreciate it every time we use it.

If you've ever ridden the Green Line to IIT, this sound is likely familiar to you!

One of my favorite parts of living in the city is the ability to be a night owl without a car. If you want to go on an adventure, time of day doesn't have to matter. This photo is one I took at Lake Red Line station a few weeks ago and it's a very gentle calm that you get at 3am.
